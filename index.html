My name is Imagine Farris and i'm a digital artist! I live in Perris, California and I started my art journey at the age of 10. My passion came from watching a bunch of cartoons; The Powerpuff Girls being my main inspiration! I enjoyed making art so much that I even had a youtube channel catered to me recording my art process, and now, 8 years later, I still make art! Everyday, I work to improve little by little to create some sort of time for myself to see how far i've come.

My artstyle focuses on the drawing of people.
